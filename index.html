Our Story

This association was created after observing the difficulties faced by many students — lack of resources, isolation, and limited peer support.

Our goal is to bring students together, provide academic and personal support, and help everyone succeed regardless of their background or field.

ASU was founded on June 14, 2025 with one simple belief: no student should be left behind. Through sharing, mutual encouragement, and a welcoming community, we continue to grow.
